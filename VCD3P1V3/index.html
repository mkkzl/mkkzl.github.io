Gitan Latin Medium

Gitan Latin was designed by Florian Runge in 2016. It is a flared, humanist sans serif which has a sculptural texture from its deeply cut wedge terminals and cuneiform head serifs. The font is appealing in its mimicry of writing with a pen. I was drawn to Gitan due to its similarity to the DSB train font from my time in Denmark.

Meghan Kozal
